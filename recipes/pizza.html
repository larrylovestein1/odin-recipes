pizza

Description

An italian dish , consisting a few simple ingrediants to make

flat circle shaped crust with sauce and cheese and or other toppings.

Ingredients

  • dough
  • sauce
  • cheese
Steps
  1. Prepare dough in circle shape on top on pizza stone
  2. Add layer of sauce!
  3. Add layer of cheese ontop of the sauce
  4. Put in oven on 400 degrees for 19-25 minutes

Enjoy!!
