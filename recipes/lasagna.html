Description

lasagna is a simple pasta , all it truly consists of is a layering of noodles, cheeses and meat

steps for cooking
  1. Spread about a cup of meat sauce into a 9x13 pan
  2. Top the noodles with some of ther cheese mixture
  3. Reaoeat the layers,ending with a layer of noodles and sauce
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. Remove foil,top with mozzarella and parmesan and bake another 15 minutes
